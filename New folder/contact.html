Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I report a lost or stolen card?

If your card is lost or stolen, please call our 24/7 helpline immediately at 0800 234 567. We will cancel your card and arrange for a replacement to be sent to you.

What should I do if I suspect fraudulent activity?

If you notice any suspicious transactions or believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at 0800 231 232. Our fraud team is available 24/7 to assist you.

How can I reset my online banking password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the online banking login page. Alternatively, you can call our support team at 0800 231 232 for assistance.

What documents do I need to open an account?

To open an account with Ulster Bank, you'll typically need proof of identity (passport or driving license) and proof of address (utility bill or bank statement from another bank). Requirements may vary depending on the account type.

How do I update my personal details?

You can update your personal details through online banking, by visiting any Ulster Bank branch, or by calling our customer service team at 0800 231 232.
