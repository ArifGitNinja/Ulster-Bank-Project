Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our loans

What is the minimum income required for a loan?

We typically require a minimum annual income of £10,000 for our personal loans. However, we consider your overall financial situation, including your credit history and existing commitments, when assessing your application.

Can I pay off my loan early?

Yes, you can pay off your Ulster Bank loan early without any early repayment charges or fees. This can save you money on interest, and there are no penalties for settling your loan ahead of schedule.

How quickly will I receive the funds?

Once your loan application is approved, we aim to transfer the funds to your account within 24 hours. In most cases, you'll receive the money on the same day if approved before 3pm on a working day.

What happens if I miss a payment?

If you're having difficulty making a payment, please contact us as soon as possible. We may be able to help by adjusting your payment date or discussing alternative arrangements. Missing payments can affect your credit score and may result in additional charges.
