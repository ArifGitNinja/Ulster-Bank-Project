Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our mortgages

How much deposit do I need for a mortgage?

For first-time buyers, we offer mortgages with a 5% deposit. For other residential mortgages, we typically require at least 10% deposit, though some products may require more. The exact amount will depend on your personal circumstances and the property value.

How long does the mortgage application process take?

Once you've submitted a full application, we aim to provide a mortgage offer within 2-3 weeks, provided we have all the necessary documentation. The overall process from application to completion typically takes 4-8 weeks, depending on the complexity of your case and the property chain.

Can I get a mortgage if I'm self-employed?

Yes, we offer mortgages to self-employed applicants. Typically, we'll need to see at least two years of accounts or SA302 forms from HMRC. We understand that self-employed income can fluctuate, so we'll work with you to find a solution that reflects your true earning potential.

What is the maximum age for a mortgage?

We consider mortgage applications where the mortgage term extends beyond the applicant's retirement age. Typically, we require the mortgage to be repaid by age 75, but we'll consider your retirement income and overall financial situation when making our assessment.
