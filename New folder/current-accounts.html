Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our current accounts

What documents do I need to open a current account?

You'll typically need proof of identity (such as a passport or driving licence) and proof of address (such as a recent utility bill or bank statement). If you're applying for a student account, you'll also need proof of your student status.

Can I switch my existing current account to Ulster Bank?

Yes, we offer a Current Account Switch Service that makes moving your account to Ulster Bank simple. We'll transfer your balance, direct debits, and standing orders within 7 working days.

Is there a fee for using my debit card abroad?

For our Standard and Student accounts, there is a 2.75% non-sterling transaction fee when using your debit card abroad. For our Reward Account, this fee is waived as part of the account benefits.

How do I set up an overdraft?

You can apply for an overdraft through our mobile app, online banking, or by visiting a branch. We'll assess your application based on your circumstances and credit history.
