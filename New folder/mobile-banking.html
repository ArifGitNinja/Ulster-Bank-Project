Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our mobile banking services

Is the Ulster Bank mobile app free to download?

Yes, the Ulster Bank mobile app is completely free to download from the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. There are no charges for using the app to manage your accounts.

What devices are compatible with the mobile banking app?

Our mobile banking app is compatible with most iOS and Android devices. For iOS, you'll need iOS 12.0 or later. For Android, you'll need Android 7.0 or later.

How do I reset my mobile banking password?

If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it directly from the app login screen by tapping "Forgot Password." You'll need to verify your identity using your registered email or phone number.

Is mobile banking secure?

Yes, we use industry-leading security measures including encryption, secure login methods, and fraud monitoring to protect your accounts and personal information.

Can I use mobile banking abroad?

Yes, you can access our mobile banking services from most countries. However, we recommend using a secure Wi-Fi connection rather than public networks when abroad for added security.
